RESPONSIBILITIES OF A CA

A Campus Ambassador is an official representative of Culfest in his/her college and is an essential link between the two. As a Campus Ambassador you will be striving to ensure maximum participation at Culfest 2018 from your college. This can be achieved through:
  • Online publicity of Culfest through social media in your city and especially in your college.
  • Offline publicity of Culfest events by sticking posters or any other means in your college.
  • Encouraging and facilitating participation from your college in the various online and offline events conducted by Culfest.
  • Helping out Culfest to reach out to your college in any manner you deem fit.
  • Word of Mouth publicity.

GUIDELINES-

As a Campus Ambassador you would be working in the below mentioned criterias. Every deed and activity has its own points allotted. Your tasks can broadly be divided into Facebook Activities and Non-Facebook Activities.
THE SCORING WAY –
  • Effective sharing of posts gives you instant lead in the CA program. Sharing of posts range from your timeline or in the groups which increase your lead. It must be noted that posts must be shared with privacy setting 'Everyone' or with 'Friends of Friends' on the timeline; failing which your lead might decrease. Increment in the number of friends who have liked the Facebook page of CULFEST would also increase your lead.
  • You would be eyed by us on weekly basis as well which means - the ambassador who is in the lead in the given week would be termed as AMBASSADOR OF THE WEEK which would fetch him extra rewards.
  • Sharing contacts details of the participants as well as professionals with us would also help in increasing your lead.
  • Greater usage of your Campus Ambassador ID, which is of utmost importance, puts you further ahead in the race of campus ambassadors.
INCENTIVES AND PERKS OF OUTPERFORMING
  • CASH REWARDS
  • SELFIE WITH THE CELEBRITY OF THE PRO NIGHT
  • FREE REGISTRATION – which includes you to permit to participate in all the events and CELEBRITY NIGHT PASS along with the goodies bag and fest Tshirt.
